Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your Pulp account and all associated data by emailing us directly.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and phone number
  • Your household address
  • All mail submissions and opt-out records
  • Any photos associated with your submissions
  • All personal suppression data tied to your account
⚠️

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your opt-out history will be lost and cannot be recovered.

To request deletion, send an email from the phone number associated with your account. We'll process your request within 7 business days.
