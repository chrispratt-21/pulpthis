A note on how this works

Unlike email, there's no legal requirement for direct mail marketers to honor opt-out requests. But that doesn't mean you're powerless. When you submit a piece of mail, we build a verified record of your opt-out request and work to get it in front of the right people at the sending company. Some mailers will honor it promptly. Others may take time. A few may not respond at all.

What we can promise: your request is logged, your voice is heard, and we're on your side.
